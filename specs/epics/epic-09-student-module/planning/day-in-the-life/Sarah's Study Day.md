**With Transcript Parser + Local AI (Ollama - Privacy Mode)** (Total: ~90 min):
Sarah is privacy-conscious and doesn't want her lecture data sent to cloud APIs.

1. **Review Biology transcript** (15 min)
   - Opens transcript, searches "Krebs cycle"
   - Clicks "Generate Flashcards" ‚Üí Uses local Ollama (LLaMA 3 8B model)
   - Processing happens on her laptop (no data sent to cloud)
   - AI creates 25 flashcards in 45 seconds (slightly slower than cloud, but private)
   - **Privacy benefit**: Lecture content never leaves her device
   - Reviews cards, exports to Anki

2. **Create Biology study guide (offline)** (10 min)
   - Clicks "Generate Study Guide (Local AI)"
   - Ollama processes locally using LLaMA 3
   - Study guide generated in 20 seconds
   - Quality is 85% as good as GPT-4, but 100% private
   - Works offline - no internet needed
   - **Cost benefit**: $0 AI costs (vs $0.05-0.10 per cloud summary)

3. **Why Sarah chooses local AI**:
   - Privacy: Her biology lecture discusses controversial topics (evolution, genetics)
   - University policy: Some professors prohibit cloud recording
   - Cost: Free tier, no AI API costs
   - Offline: Works in library basement with no Wi-Fi
   - Trust: Data never sent to Google/OpenAI/Anthropic

## üéØ Implications for Design

### Must-Have Features (Based on Sarah's Day)
1. **Fast AI processing** - Summaries available within minutes of lecture end
2. **Mobile-first design** - Sarah studies 40% on phone
3. **One-click flashcards** - No manual creation needed
4. **Spaced repetition integration** - Automatic review reminders
5. **Search across all lectures** - Find concepts quickly
6. **Export to Anki/Quizlet** - Works with tools she already uses

### User Flow Priorities
1. Record lecture ‚Üí Get summary ‚Üí Generate flashcards (< 2 min total)
2. Search for concept ‚Üí Find in transcript ‚Üí Create quiz (< 1 min)
3. Mobile: Quick flashcard review during breaks (< 5 min)

### Metrics to Track
- **Time saved per lecture** (target: 60+ min)
- **Flashcard generation speed** (target: < 30 sec)
- **Search accuracy** (target: 95%+ relevance)
- **Mobile usage %** (expect 30-50%)
- **User satisfaction** (target: 8+/10)
