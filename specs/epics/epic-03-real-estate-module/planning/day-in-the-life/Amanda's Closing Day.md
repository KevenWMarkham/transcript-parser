  - Sarah: "Great, and you'll fix the ice maker in the fridge, right?"

- 8 hours of legal review, $3,500 in legal fees
- Settlement: $400 paid to Sarah (compromise), everyone unhappy
- **Loss**: $3,500 legal + damaged relationships + 10 hours of Amanda's time

- Title company's insurance investigates, takes 2 months, $15K legal fees
- Turns out borrower's email was hacked, but Amanda can't prove she gave correct wire instructions
- **Outcome**: $15K legal fees, 2 months stress, near-miss on $680K fraud

#### With Transcript Parser + Ollama ‚úÖ

- Borrower: "Thanks for confirming. I'll ignore that email and wire to 7823 as we discussed."
- Wire fraud prevented, $680K saved
- Amanda reports to FBI (transcript used as evidence)
- **Saved**: $680,000, provided proof of correct wire instructions

## üéØ Implications for Design

### Must-Have Features for Title/Escrow Officers

1. **Local AI (Ollama) - MANDATORY** - Legal/financial data cannot go to cloud
2. **Multi-Speaker Identification** - Label who said what (6-8 people in closings)
3. **SoftPro Integration** - Export closing notes to title software
4. **Dollar Amount Extraction** - AI finds "$800 holdback," "$150 credit" in transcript
5. **Secure Transcript Sharing** - Send to all parties post-closing (encrypted)
6. **Search by Agreement Type** - "Show all fence agreements" or "appliance credits"
7. **Wire Instruction Verification** - Timestamp when wire instructions given
8. **Retention Policy** - Auto-archive after 7 years (state law compliance)

### User Flow Priorities

**Flow 1: Closing Meeting**
1. Setup ‚Üí Start recording ‚Üí Announce to parties (transparency)
2. Closing proceeds ‚Üí Multi-speaker capture ‚Üí Verbal agreements recorded
3. Finish ‚Üí Review AI summary ‚Üí Verify all agreements captured

**Flow 2: Post-Closing Distribution**
1. Export summary to SoftPro ‚Üí One-click
2. Generate transcript excerpt ‚Üí Share with all parties (encrypted email)
3. Archive full recording ‚Üí 7-year retention

**Flow 3: Dispute Prevention**
1. Party claims: "That's not what we agreed!"
2. Search transcript: "Fence" or "Ice maker" or "$800"
3. Pull exact quote ‚Üí Play for parties
4. Dispute resolved in minutes ‚Üí $0 legal fees

### Metrics to Track

- **Primary**: Post-closing disputes (target: 0/year)
- **Secondary**: Legal fees (target: <$2K/year, down from $35K)
- **Tertiary**: Time per closing (target: <3.5 hrs)
- **Wire Fraud**: Incidents prevented (track verification requests)
- **Client Satisfaction**: Referrals from agents (target: >80%)
