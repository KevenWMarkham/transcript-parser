## ðŸ˜“ Pain Points & Frustrations

### Current Work Challenges
1. **Language Complexity**
   - Landlords/investors use complex financial jargon (cap rates, NOI, cash-on-cash return)
   - Legal documents and fair housing rules in formal English
   - Fast-paced HOA meetings with native English speakers
   - Worried about misunderstanding important details
   - Accent sometimes creates communication barriers with vendors

2. **Tenant Communication**
   - Tenants speak 3 different languages (English, Spanish, Haitian Creole)
   - Complaints often emotional, need accurate record for legal protection
   - "He-said-she-said" disputes require precise documentation
   - Late-night emergency calls hard to document accurately

3. **Compliance & Liability**
   - Fair Housing complaints require exact records of conversations
   - Maintenance requests must be documented (liability if injury occurs)
   - Eviction proceedings need accurate timeline of communication
   - Cannot afford mistakes in legal documentation

4. **Cultural Challenges**
   - Some tenants distrust "official" recording (immigration fears)
   - Privacy concerns in low-income communities
   - Needs discrete recording to avoid making people uncomfortable
   - Spanish-speaking staff need information in their language

### Technology Pain Points
- Uses phone recorder for important calls (audio only, hard to review)
- Handwritten notes during inspections (messy, time-consuming)
- Google Translate for complex English terms (loses context)
- Manually translates meeting notes to Spanish for staff (2-3 hrs/week)
- Worried about cloud storage of tenant conversations (privacy)
- No systematic way to search past tenant interactions

## ðŸŽ“ Typical Use Cases

### Weekly Property Management Workflow
1. **Tenant Complaint Calls** (5-10 per week):
   - Records call for liability protection
   - Reviews transcript (easier than re-listening)
   - Translates key points to Spanish for maintenance staff
   - Creates work order with exact tenant description

2. **Property Inspections** (2-3 buildings/week):
   - Walks units, records observations hands-free
   - Notes maintenance issues, safety concerns, lease violations
   - Generates inspection report from transcript
   - Shares with owners and maintenance team

3. **HOA/Board Meetings** (monthly):
   - Records fast-paced English discussions
   - Reviews transcript to understand complex decisions
   - Looks up unfamiliar business terms
   - Creates action items for follow-up

4. **Landlord Investor Meetings** (weekly):
   - Records financial discussions (NOI, expenses, cap ex)
   - Reviews transcript to understand numbers correctly
   - Creates summary report for owners
   - Improves understanding of real estate finance terminology

### Specific Scenarios
- **Tenant dispute mediation**: Records both sides, generates neutral summary
- **Maintenance vendor calls**: Records scope of work, pricing, timeline
- **Lease renewal negotiations**: Accurate record of terms discussed
- **Emergency calls**: Late-night plumbing/AC issues documented accurately

## ðŸŽ¨ Design Implications

### UI/UX Preferences
- **Mobile-first** - Must work flawlessly on Android phone
- **Discrete recording** - No obvious indicators during tenant calls
- **Bilingual interface** - Spanish UI option
- **Offline capable** - Many buildings have poor cell signal
- **Privacy-focused** - Local storage, no cloud requirement
- **Simple controls** - Quick start/stop for emergency calls

### Feature Priorities (Ranked)
1. **Local AI translation (Ollama)** - English â†” Spanish, privacy-preserving
2. **Bilingual transcripts** - Side-by-side English/Spanish
3. **Discrete recording** - No visible indicators, tenant privacy
4. **Search tenant history** - Find previous complaints/requests
5. **WhatsApp integration** - Transcribe voice messages automatically
6. **Inspection report generation** - Auto-create reports from walkthrough notes
7. **Business vocabulary learning** - Real estate/legal term flashcards

### Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid)
- **Cloud-required features** - Privacy concerns for tenant data
- **Complex setup** - Needs to work immediately during emergency calls
- **English-only interface** - Must offer Spanish option
- **Expensive subscription** - Budget-conscious small business owner
- **Desktop-required features** - She's never at a desk
- **Visible recording indicators** - Creates distrust with privacy-sensitive tenants
