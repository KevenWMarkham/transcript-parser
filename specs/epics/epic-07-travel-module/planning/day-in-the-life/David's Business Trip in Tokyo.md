## üéØ Implications for Design

### Must-Have Features (Based on David's Day)

1. **AirPods/Bluetooth Audio Recording**: Hands-free capture during walking/multitasking
2. **Business Intelligence Extraction**: Auto-flag competitive insights, timing windows, pain points
3. **CRM Export**: Pull client details, personal info, business context
4. **Expense Report Generation**: Date, merchant, amount, purpose, attendees from conversations
5. **Instagram Caption Generator**: Create posts from transcript highlights
6. **City-Based Knowledge**: "Show all Tokyo recommendations from all trips"
7. **Relationship Tracking**: Link conversations to LinkedIn contacts, flag follow-up points
8. **Multi-Language Support**: Japanese-English conversation handling
9. **Research/Reading List Extraction**: Auto-pull mentioned papers, books, articles
10. **Business Lead Flagging**: Identify partnership opportunities, sales timing, introductions

### User Flow Priorities

**Morning**: 30-second review of yesterday's recommendations
**During Day**: Zero-touch recording via AirPods (meetings, transportation, meals)
**Evening**: 15-minute review (expense export, content creation, CRM updates, tomorrow planning)
**Monthly**: Expense report export (10 minutes vs. 4 hours)
**Quarterly**: City knowledge review (becoming expert in frequently visited cities)

### Metrics to Track

- **Time Savings**: 2+ hours/day on note-taking and admin
- **Expense Report Speed**: <15 minutes/month (vs. 4+ hours)
- **Content Engagement**: 3-5x Instagram likes from authentic stories
- **Follow-Up Response Rate**: 75%+ (vs. 30% with generic outreach)
- **Business Development**: Leads captured and converted to opportunities
- **Repeat Visit Efficiency**: 50%+ time savings on trip planning (use past recommendations)
- **Network Depth**: Quality connections vs. shallow business card exchanges
