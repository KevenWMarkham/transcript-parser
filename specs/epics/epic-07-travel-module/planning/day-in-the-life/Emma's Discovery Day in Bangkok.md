## üéØ Implications for Design

### Must-Have Features (Based on Emma's Day)

1. **Background Recording**: Pocket/table recording without phone manipulation
2. **Multi-Language Detection**: Auto-detect Thai + English mixing
3. **Recommendation Extraction**: Auto-identify restaurants, guesthouses, activities with context
4. **Anki Export**: One-click flashcard generation with audio clips
5. **Photo-Transcript Matching**: Link photos to conversation context by timestamp
6. **GPS Auto-Tagging**: Pin mentioned places automatically
7. **Daily Summary**: End-of-day recap of what was captured
8. **Offline Mode**: Record without WiFi, sync later
9. **Low Battery Usage**: All-day recording on single charge

### User Flow Priorities

**Morning**: Quick-start recording in hostel common room (1 tap, phone on table)
**During Day**: Passive recording in pocket during tours/transport
**Afternoon**: Quick review at coffee shop (20-min productive session)
**Evening**: Daily summary review before bed, create flashcards for tomorrow's practice

### Metrics to Track

- **Recommendation Capture Rate**: Target 90%+ (vs. current ~40%)
- **Language Retention**: 75%+ flashcard success rate with audio context
- **Time Saved**: 10+ hours/week freed up
- **Content Quality**: 3x Instagram engagement from authentic stories
- **Money Saved**: $300+/month from better local recommendations
- **Stress Reduction**: From 7/10 ‚Üí 3/10 anxiety about forgetting
