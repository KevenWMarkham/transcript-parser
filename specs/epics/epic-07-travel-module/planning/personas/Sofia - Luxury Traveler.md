## ðŸŽ¨ Design Implications

### UI/UX Preferences

**Luxury Aesthetic**:
- Elegant, minimal design (think Aesop, not TikTok)
- Premium typography and spacing
- Sophisticated color palette (gold, deep navy, cream)
- Smooth animations, premium feel
- No "consumer app" vibes

**Mobile-First, Content-Focused**:
- Large, beautiful thumbnails
- Easy screenshot/share from within app
- Quick access to recent recordings
- Swipe gestures over taps (more elegant)

**Privacy & Discretion**:
- Face ID/passcode required
- Private mode for sensitive conversations
- No social sharing unless explicit
- Elegant "recording" indicator (discreet, not flashy)

**Integration with Content Workflow**:
- Export to Instagram/clipboard with formatting
- Link to photos in Camera Roll by timestamp
- Beautiful PDF exports for client sharing

### Feature Priorities (Ranked)

1. **Automatic transcription with speaker identification** (chef, sommelier, guide)
2. **Instagram caption generation** from transcript highlights
3. **Proper name extraction** (people, places, wines, dishes, brands)
4. **Multi-language support** (English, French, Italian, Swedish)
5. **Photo-transcript timeline matching**
6. **Person tagging & relationship tracking** ("from Francesco at Hotel Eden")
7. **City-based recommendation filtering**
8. **Beautiful PDF exports** for client recommendations
9. **Privacy controls** (client vs. personal, public vs. private)
10. **Follow-up reminders** based on conversation content
11. **Voice to text for quick dictation** during getting ready
12. **Hashtag & location tag suggestions**
13. **Calendar integration** for seasonal recommendations
14. **Contact linking** for network management

### Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid)

**Aesthetic Violations**:
- Consumer-grade design (needs premium feel)
- Cluttered interfaces (clean, spacious layouts only)
- Playful fonts or colors (sophisticated only)
- Generic stock icons (custom, elegant iconography)

**Workflow Disruption**:
- Requiring desktop for key features (she's mobile-first)
- Complex manual tagging (needs automation)
- Slow processing (needs same-day turnaround for content)
- Interrupting experiences (recording must be invisible)

**Privacy & Security**:
- No encryption option (client info is sensitive)
- Automatic cloud backup without control (needs privacy)
- No deletion options (must be able to erase sensitive content)
- Social features that leak her network contacts

**Content Creation Friction**:
- Plain text exports (needs formatted, shareable output)
- No photo integration (transcript is useless without visual context)
- Can't customize branding on exports
- No templates for different content types (Story vs. Post vs. Reel)

**Professional Limitations**:
- Can't separate client vs. personal content
- No professional export formats (PDF, branded lists)
- Missing attribution ("source: [person] at [place]")
- No way to track recommendation success rate
