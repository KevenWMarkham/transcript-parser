## ðŸŽ¨ Design Implications

### UI/UX Preferences

**Professional, Reliable Tool**:
- Not flashy, just functional
- Business-focused aesthetics
- Clear ROI indicators ("You've saved 8 hours this week")

**Speed & Efficiency**:
- Quick capture (one tap to start tour recording)
- Fast sharing (one tap to send recommendations)
- Batch operations (send to all 12 guests at once)

**Mobile-First, Offline-Capable**:
- Android + iOS support essential
- Works in Old Town with spotty signal
- Low battery usage (phone in pocket all day)
- Auto-sync when WiFi available

**B2B Features**:
- Multi-user (Marco + 2 guides, shared knowledge base)
- Client management (track repeat guests)
- Analytics dashboard (monthly performance review)
- Training mode (mark tours for guide reference)

### Feature Priorities (Ranked)

1. **Auto-transcription of tours** with background recording
2. **Recommendation extraction & categorization** (food/drink/sights/practical)
3. **One-click guest sharing** (personalized email with recommendations)
4. **FAQ question tracking** (what's asked repeatedly?)
5. **Performance analytics** (engagement by tour section)
6. **Training library** (save best tours for new guide learning)
7. **Client CRM** (track preferences, past conversations)
8. **Safety documentation** (allergy info, accessibility notes)
9. **Multi-language support** (Czech, English, German, Russian)
10. **Audio guide creation** (convert tour transcripts to scripts)
11. **Review automation** (post-tour review request emails)
12. **Offline mode** (record tours in medieval cellars with no signal)
13. **Multi-guide collaboration** (shared recommendation database)
14. **Analytics exports** (monthly business review reports)

### Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid)

**Complexity**:
- Steep learning curve (needs to work from day 1)
- Desktop-required features (he's mobile-first)
- Manual tagging/organization (no time between tours)
- Complex analytics (needs simple dashboards, not data science)

**Business Misalignment**:
- Consumer-focused design (this is a business tool)
- Social features (doesn't want to share tours publicly)
- No multi-user support (has 2 guides who need access)
- Lack of CRM features (repeat clients are 15% of revenue)

**Platform Limitations**:
- iOS-only (uses Android)
- Cloud-dependent (needs offline for tours)
- No export options (wants data ownership)
- Subscription only (seasonal business, prefers annual payment)

**Tour Guide Workflow**:
- Requires phone in hand (needs pocket recording)
- Interrupts tour flow (can't be fidgeting with app)
- Battery drain (8-hour tour days)
- Storage hog (limited phone space)

**Privacy & Legal**:
- No consent management (recording clients requires disclosure)
- Can't delete sensitive data (GDPR compliance)
- No data retention controls (how long to keep tour recordings?)
- Unclear liability (is recorded safety info admissible if disputed?)
